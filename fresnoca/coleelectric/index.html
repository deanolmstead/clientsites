Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Quick answers to what Fresno homeowners and business owners ask us most.

Are you licensed and insured?
Yes. Cole Electric, LLC is a fully licensed California electrical contractor and carries comprehensive liability and workers' compensation insurance. Our CSLB license number is on file and displayed on every invoice.
Do you offer free estimates?
Yes. We provide free, no-obligation estimates for all residential and commercial electrical projects in the Fresno area. Call (559) 298-6464 or submit our contact form to schedule yours.
How quickly can you schedule a service call?
For most residential service calls in Fresno and surrounding cities, we can schedule within 1–3 business days. Larger commercial projects are scheduled based on scope and current workload. Call us for current availability.
Do you handle both residential and commercial electrical work?
Absolutely. Our team handles everything from single-outlet residential repairs to full commercial tenant improvements, panel upgrades, EV charger installations, lighting design, and code-compliant rewires.
What forms of payment do you accept?
We accept cash, check, and all major credit cards. Payment terms for larger commercial jobs are typically discussed and agreed upon during the estimate phase.
