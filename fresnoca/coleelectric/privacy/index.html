Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Information We Collect

When you submit a contact form on our website, we collect the information you voluntarily provide, including: your name, phone number, email address, the type of electrical service you're inquiring about, and any project details you choose to share.

We do not collect or store any payment, banking, or government-issued identification information through this website.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit solely to:

  • Respond to your service inquiry or estimate request
  • Schedule appointments and follow up on completed work
  • Provide quotes and project documentation
  • Communicate with you about ongoing or future electrical services you request

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Form Submission Provider

Our contact forms are processed by Web3Forms, a third-party form delivery service that securely transmits submissions to our email inbox. Web3Forms does not retain submission data beyond the time required for delivery. You can review their data handling practices at web3forms.com/privacy.

Your California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with the following rights regarding the personal information we collect about you:

  • Right to Know — You can request a summary of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Delete — You can request that we delete the personal information we hold about you, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Right to Opt Out — We do not sell personal information, but you may request that we limit how we use what you have shared.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination — We will not deny you services or charge you a different price for exercising any of these rights.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Data Retention

We retain inquiry information only as long as necessary to provide the service you requested and to maintain reasonable business records (typically 3 years from last contact). After that period, we delete or anonymize the information.

Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising pixels. Browser local storage is used only to remember your light/dark mode preference on this device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects when changes were last made. Continued use of this website after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
