Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Halsey Electric Inc ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information submitted through our website at halseyelectric.com (the "Site"). It also describes your rights as a California resident under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA).

Information We Collect

When you submit our contact form, we collect the information you provide voluntarily, including:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • The service you're requesting and any project details you choose to share

We do not use cookies, analytics scripts, advertising trackers, or third-party fingerprinting on this Site.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit solely to respond to your inquiry, schedule estimates or service appointments, and provide the electrical services you've requested. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Form Submission Provider

Our contact form is processed by Web3Forms, a form-handling service that delivers your submission to our business email. Web3Forms processes your submission according to its own privacy policy. We do not authorize Web3Forms to use your information for any purpose other than delivering your message to us.

Your CCPA / CPRA Rights

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Know — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Right to Delete — request that we delete personal information we have collected from you
  • Right to Correct — request that we correct inaccurate personal information
  • Right to Opt Out — opt out of any sale or sharing of your personal information (we do not sell or share)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination — exercise these rights without receiving discriminatory treatment

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information at the bottom of this page.

Data Retention

We retain your inquiry information only as long as necessary to provide the requested services and to maintain reasonable business records. Inquiry data not resulting in active business is purged on a routine basis.

Cookies

This Site does not set first-party tracking cookies. Embedded Google Maps content (in our contact section) may set cookies governed by Google's privacy policy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the Site after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
