Common questions

Before You Call,
Here's What Folks Ask.

Are you licensed and insured in California?
Yes. Halsey Electric Inc is fully licensed by the California Contractors State License Board (CSLB) and carries general liability insurance and workers' compensation. Our license number is available upon request.
Do you offer free estimates?
Yes — we provide free written estimates for all residential and commercial electrical projects. We'll assess the scope of work, answer your questions, and give you a clear price before any work begins.
What types of electrical work do you handle?
Panel upgrades and replacements, residential and commercial wiring, EV charger installation, recessed and accent lighting, circuit additions, safety inspections, and troubleshooting. If it involves electricity in your home or business, we can help.
How quickly can you respond to a service request?
We typically schedule service calls within one to three business days. For time-sensitive projects, we make every effort to accommodate urgent requests. Call us directly at (559) 291-6226 for fastest response.
Do you work on both residential and commercial properties?
Yes. We serve homeowners, landlords, property managers, and commercial businesses throughout the Fresno area. Whether it's a single-family home, apartment complex, retail space, or office building, our team has the expertise and licensing to get it done right.

Panel Upgrades & Replacements

  • Sub-panels & meter combos
  • Whole-home surge protection
  • Permitted & inspected
